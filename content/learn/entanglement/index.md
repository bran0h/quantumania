---
title: Entanglement
description: See how two qubits can become linked in ways classical bits never can.
---

So far each qubit lived on its own. With two qubits, the combined system has **four basis states**: |00⟩, |01⟩, |10⟩, and |11⟩.

**Entanglement** is when the joint state cannot be split into separate descriptions for each qubit. Measuring one instantly tells you about the other — a correlation with no classical equivalent.

In this chapter you will:

- Explore the four two-qubit basis states
- Build the Bell state |Φ+⟩ with H and CNOT
- See how entangled measurements differ from product states
