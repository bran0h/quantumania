---
title: Measuring Entanglement
description: Compare entangled and product states through joint measurements.
---

Entanglement shows up in **joint measurements**. For the Bell state, q0 and q1 are individually random (50/50) but always agree — you never see |01⟩ or |10⟩.

Compare that to the product state |+⟩|0⟩: q0 is random but q1 is always 0. Same marginals, different correlations.

Measure both qubits repeatedly in each mode and watch which outcomes appear in the histogram.
