---
title: Quantum Gates
description: Learn how operations rotate and flip qubit states — the building blocks of quantum circuits.
---

Classical computers use logic gates (AND, OR, NOT) to process bits. Quantum computers use **quantum gates** to transform qubit states before measurement.

In this chapter you will:

- Read simple circuit diagrams with gates on a qubit wire
- Apply Pauli X and Z gates and see how they change states
- Use the Hadamard gate to create superposition
