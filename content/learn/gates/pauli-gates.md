The **Pauli X** gate is the quantum NOT gate: it swaps |0⟩ and |1⟩. Think of it as a bit flip on the Bloch sphere.

The **Pauli Z** gate flips the phase of |1⟩ (|1⟩ → −|1⟩) without changing measurement probabilities in the standard basis. Phase is a hidden property that matters when gates are combined.
