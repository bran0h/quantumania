The **Hadamard gate (H)** is one of the most important gates in quantum computing. It turns definite states into superpositions:

- H|0⟩ = |+⟩ — equal mix of 0 and 1
- H|1⟩ = |−⟩ — equal mix with a negative phase on |1⟩

This is how quantum algorithms create the interference patterns they need. Try both starting states below and watch the probabilities and Bloch vector update.
