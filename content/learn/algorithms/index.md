title: Quantum Algorithms
description: See how quantum circuits solve specific problems faster than classical approaches.
---

You now know qubits, gates, and entanglement. **Quantum algorithms** put those pieces together to solve specific problems with fewer steps than classical computers — but only for the right kind of problem.

This is not a universal "quantum computer is always faster" story. Quantum speedups appear when interference and structure can be exploited — like telling whether a function is constant or balanced, or finding a needle in a haystack with fewer queries.

In this chapter you will:

- Compare classical vs quantum query counts for two famous examples
- Run the Deutsch–Jozsa circuit and read its constant/balanced verdict
- Watch Grover's algorithm amplify a marked search result
