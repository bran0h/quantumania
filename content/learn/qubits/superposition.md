title: Superposition
description: Explore how a qubit can hold both 0 and 1 at the same time — until you look.
---

**Superposition** means a qubit is in a weighted combination of |0⟩ and |1⟩ at the same time. We describe this with amplitudes; when you measure, those amplitudes turn into probabilities.

The state |+⟩ is an equal superposition: 50% chance of 0 and 50% chance of 1. States |0⟩ and |1⟩ are not superpositions — they are definite basis states.
