Welcome to your first chapter. A **qubit** is the basic unit of quantum information — the quantum version of a classical bit. But unlike a bit that must be 0 or 1, a qubit can do something strange and powerful called **superposition**.

In this chapter you will:

- Compare classical bits with qubits side by side
- See what superposition looks like as probabilities
- Measure a qubit and watch random outcomes build a histogram
